Team Strengths

Our team has a solid foundation of front-end and back-end technologies, having worked on many different projects. Many of our members have extensive experience working with Java. Furthermore, our team has a strong understanding of Android and Web development. Another technical strength of our team, is our ability to create and work with databases, as well as working with NodeJs. All members of our team also have experience following agile methodology, specifically scrum. We have prior experience working within teams, to complete tasks before given deadlines.

Team Goals

Throughout the project, our team has set goals to help deliver a quality product, and to help develop as professionals. With regards, to the project we aim to learn how to develop and implement user interfaces that are effective, clean, and efficient for others to use. Another one of our goals is to create a product that is not simply just a school assignment, but one that can be used by used in real world situations by clients. Furthermore, we want to develop our ability to quickly adapt to any requirements that may change throughout the course of this project. One last goal we have is to improve our ability working with different technologies, learning from and aiding each other.
